# CNBE-32: Strategic Vision

## The Big Picture

CNBE-32 is not a better Chinese encoding -- it is an exploration of a technological path that could change how 1.4 billion people interact with AI.

## Three Beliefs

1. **Chinese structural wisdom is AI's natural nutrient** -- The thousands of years of Chinese character evolution encode a world model
2. **Hardware-native support is the path forward** -- RISC-V gives us the chance to redefine the instruction set
3. **AI factories break down professional barriers** -- Everyone defines rules in their native language

## Ultimate Goal

Make Chinese a native language of AI, so that all Chinese speakers can enter the AI era with native language thinking.
